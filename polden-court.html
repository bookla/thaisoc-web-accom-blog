Polden Court

Published 14 May 2022

Room & Flatmates

Polden court provides 3 options of bedroom to choose between en-suite small rooms (single bed), en-suite rooms (small-double bed) and large en-suite rooms (double bed). For me, I live in a normal en-suite room which provides a medium size room, plenty of storage, a kettle, desk and chair, my own mini fridge and a bath tub in my bathroom. Including me, there are around 10 people sharing 2 big kitchens in the same flat.

Room

En-suite Bathroom

Facilities

As we have our own mini fridge in our room, the fridge in the main kitchens are quite small but people don’t usually put their stuffs in the main fridge anyway. Both kitchens have a freezer, toaster, and microwave. Polden court also has a big study lounge in front of the building.

We share a launderette with Westwood accommodation which takes around 3 mins walk from Polden court.

Location

West side of the campus, a seven minutes walk to the library and around 4 minutes to Fresh.

Pricing

Around 230 per week (included Eat and Drink Credit)

*Eat and drink credit is compulsory and it will be in your library card which can be used with any canteen/cafe/shop in Uni except Fresh*

Final Mentions

Overall, I would recommend Polden Court according to all facilities. The room is really nice and I can’t really hear anything from the room next to me. Eat and drink credit will be stored in your library card even after you move out.

InTouch Pathanasap (Zen)
